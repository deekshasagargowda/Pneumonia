What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is a serious infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. It can cause fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.

Causes of Pneumonia

  • Viral Infections
  • Bacterial Infections
  • Fungal Infections
  • Chronic Lung Diseases
  • Smoking and Pollution
